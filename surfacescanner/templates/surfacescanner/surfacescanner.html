ARALIA - 3D IILUMINATOR

Quick and accurate

3D surface imaging

A low-cost handheld device that attaches to a regular smartphone to reveal detailed, high-resolution, 3D colour images of any surface to a depth of 5mm.

Solving an industry problem

Aralia's 3D Illuminator can enable condition monitoring (CM) and Non-Destructive testing (NDT) professionals to quickly and accurately identify surface defects as a first step in the testing process. It is easy to use on site for initial assessments which determine if additional resources or more rigorous testing is required, making it a useful aid.

To keep large assets operational, companies rely on NDT and CM regimes to determine the need for maintenance or repairs. This extends asset lifespan, reducing the need for capital investment to replace integral equipment and prevents operational failures. Over 25,000 inspections are carried out every day in the UK.

How does it work?

How does it work?

The 3D Illuminator uses Photometric Stereo (PS) illumination to capture and process images, either locally on the phone, via the cloud and transmitted off site for a remote assessment by expert review or automated via Machine Learning, facilitating archiving, displaying and processing. Offsite processing capabilities are critical during pandemics such as COVID-19.

The 3D Illuninator works ‘out of the box’ with minimal training and all necessary software easy to download.

Its simplicity and low price point enables high volumes to be readily distributed whenever necessary.
