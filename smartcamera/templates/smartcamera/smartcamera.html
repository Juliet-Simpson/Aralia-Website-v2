SMART RAIL SAFETY

real time trespass detection

Aralia’s smart anti-trespassing system is a low-cost solution that can be deployed in remote or challenging locations to reliably monitor and report rail trespassing.

Developed from technology widely deployed on large rail networks, Aralia’s modular system integrates a range of sensors together with AI classification algorithms to reliably detect potentially life-threatening or disruptive trespass incidents. The technologies embedded within the system can differentiate between trespass and other events, such as trains, track equipment and animals. It will also recognize authorised site workers by their safety clothing.

Sensitive in low light conditions, Aralia’s stand-alone solution can operate remotely without access to power or WiFi. For greater situational awareness, the system will continuously monitor pre-defined areas around railway platforms, including trackside areas adjacent to any platform.

Security personnel are quickly notified of any incidents via email or text, including a description of the trespasser. If required, the system retains forensic evidence for prosecution.

Weekly reports are generated for staff to review the frequency and nature of trespass activity. Audio-visual deterrence is based upon the characteristics of the trespass.

Remote workstations can monitor and configure all trespass detection systems that have been deployed and interface directly with other surveillance cameras.

Aralia’s smart trespassing system supports all interface standards widely adopted throughout the surveillance industry (ONVIF). The surveillance framework is currently in operation at a large number of rail locations, including platforms, trackside and rail depots. All technologies incorporated within the solution have been assessed in a rail environment.

With Aralia’s SC-01A AI empowered smart camera it is possible to do advanced analytics at the edge. Utilising a 1/2” CMOS active pixel image sensor array the SC-01A has a maximum resolution 8 MP and provides outstanding details in challenging environments. The aluminium housing allows for the system to run in harsh environments. With SC-01A’s on-board AI co-processor, advanced and bespoke analytics can provide alarms and meta-data to Aralia’s advanced visual surveillance system or other ONVIF enabled products.

The SC-01A has a simple but secure web application which enables quick setup.

  • 8MP resolution at 24fps
  • 1/2” sensor
  • Electronic image stabilisation
  • Bespoke CNN models which include the ability to detect people, luggage, trains and many other objects.
